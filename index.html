Our Restaurant

Cafe Beyond Was Originally Opened To Financially Support BEYOD-Nepal; An NGO Founded By Local And International Youth In 2009, And Is One Of The Firsts In The Locality.

Since Then, Cafe Beyond Has Evolved Into A Popular Eatery For Tourists visiting Bhaktapur. Combining It's unique Blend Of Korean-fusion And Western Food, Cafe Beyond Has Been Satisfying The Palettes For Many Visitors Throughout The Years! Because Of It's Unique Blend Of Food And Great Ambience, Cafe beyond Has turned Into A Reputable Name In Bhaktapur.
